€ddie$

Price

Starting from Wed, 24 May 2023 07:10:25 GMT, each year you just add "(" to the beginning and ") + 21%" to the end of the formula to get the price.

Year one

In year one 1 €ddie$ is priced at (21 EUR + 21 USD)* 1 + 21% VAT + 21% INFLATION. Each year this formula goes up with + 21%, just like the rings on a tree it keeps growing annually.

Year two

One year after the first €ddie$ is created the price is now ((21 EUR + 21 USD)* 1 + 21% + 21%) + 21%.

Year three

Two years after the first €ddie$ is created the price is now (((21 EUR + 21 USD)* 1 + 21% + 21%) + 21%) + 21%.

Year four

Three years after the first €ddie$ is created the price is now ((((21 EUR + 21 USD)* 1 + 21% + 21%) + 21%) + 21%) + 21% etc.

Inflation

€ddie$ price increase is a reaction to currency inflation, €ddie$ are deflationary by nature.

Timebanking

€ddie$ can also be use to pay people for their time, 60 €$ is one hour.
